Mastering Pull-Ups

Pull-ups are a fundamental exercise that target multiple muscle groups, including the back, arms, and core. Proper form is crucial for maximizing the benefits and minimizing the risk of injury.

Weighted Pull-Ups

To intensify your training, weighted pull-ups can be incorporated. This involves adding additional weight with a belt or vest, increasing the challenge and stimulating muscle growth. Start with lighter weights and progressively increase as you develop strength and maintain good form.
